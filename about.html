About Us

Founded in 2017, Farah Petker Studio is an international team of architectural creatives seeking to establish respectful, simple and sophisticated spaces as our core values of design.

Our projects span a range of global locations, scale and typologies.
Our small hands-on team takes on a limited number of projects per year as we believe in creating a relationship and bond with each project and client, paying respect, thoughtful consideration and special attention to place, light, senses, finishes and detailing. Our works cover all stages of a project’s development from concept design through to construction consultancy services.

Using current software and technology as well as coordinating with specialists on a macro and micro scale, we seek to effectively communicate and deliver our vision.
