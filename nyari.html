Nyari

Located in the vibrant city of Nairobi, Kenya, this luxury residential villa embodies a captivating fusion of elegance and innovation. 

The villa's interior layout is characterised by a series of crisp planar elements that define the various internal spaces. These elements not only lend a distinctive visual appeal but also contribute to the organisation and flow of the residence. The careful arrangement of these spaces around a protected internal court allows for an intimate and tranquil setting, providing a haven of serenity within the bustling cityscape.
